FIZZBUZZ: A Coding Project

Two numbers are entered representing "Fizz" and "Buzz".
The "Fizz" number is usually smaller than the "Buzz".

The Javascript program displays 100 numbers, 1 to 100.
"Fizz" is substituted for numbers divisible by the "Fizz" value.
"Buzz" is substituted for numbers divisible by the "Buzz" value.
Numbers divisible by both the "Fizz" value and the "Buzz" value are represented by "FizzBuzz" in BOLD

Instructions

Enter a "Fizz" and "Buzz" value.

Press the 'Start' button and the values will appear.

Press the 'Reset' button to start again.
