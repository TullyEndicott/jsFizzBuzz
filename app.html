FIZZBUZZ: A Coding Project

Two numbers are entered. The first smaller than the second. The Javascript program displays the numbers.
"Fizz" is substituted for umbers divisible by the 3.
"Buzz" is substituted for numbers divisible by 5.
Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 are represented by "FizzBuzz" in BOLD

Instructions

Enter a starting and ending value.

Press the 'Start' button and the values will appear.

Press the 'Reset' button to start again.
